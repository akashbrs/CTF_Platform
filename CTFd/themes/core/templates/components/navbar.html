DR. G. ANAND KUMAR

DEAN OF DEPARTMENT

CYBERSECURITY

We proudly recognize the invaluable support of Dr. G. Anand Kumar, Dean of Cyber Security at Malla Reddy University, Hyderabad. His vision, leadership, and encouragement have been instrumental in organizing this Capture The Flag (CTF) in such an impressive way.

Dr. Anand Kumar has consistently nurtured and guided students to excel in the dynamic field of cybersecurity, blending academic rigor with practical exposure. Under his stewardship, the department emphasizes hands-on learning through internships, projects, and industry collaborations, while also fostering teamwork, communication, and personal development.

His commitment to creating a supportive environment for research, teaching, and innovation has laid the foundation for initiatives like this CTF. We are deeply grateful for his guidance, which has empowered us to bring this event to life and inspire future cybersecurity professionals.
